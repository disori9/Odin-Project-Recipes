A pork recipe bursting in flavors from the distinct taste of bagoong mixed with other delicious spices! Best enjoyed with rice.

    Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Water
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Eggplant, Tomato
  • Onion, Garlic, Green Chili
  • Bagoong Alamang
  • Coconut Cream
  • Vinegar
  • MAGGI Magic Sarap
  • Brown Sugar

    Steps

  1. Simmer pork in water until tender. Strain and set aside. Reserve 1 cup of the pork stock.
  2. Slice pork and fry in oil until golden brown. Set aside. Fry eggplant in the same oil until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. Remove oil from the pan leaving 2 tbsp. Sauté garlic, onion, tomato, chili and bagoong. Pour vinegar. Stir in reserved pork stock and season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP® and sugar.
  4. Cut pork into pieces and add to the sauce. Pour in coconut cream and simmer for 2mins. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
